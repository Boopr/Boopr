Help
Our site is made for looking a cute pictures of pups! Please use this guide to get you started.

Add a New Dog

You can add a new dog by clicking the profile button in the Navigation bar. Then click the Add Dog button to enter the information of your dog.

Edit existing dogs / Add Images to Dog

You can edit an existing dog you own by clicking the profile button in the Navigation bar. Then click the Dog image of the dog you want to change or add images to.

Boop dogs!

Clicking any dog image on the main page will bring you to a dogs profile, there you can click on images on the How I Look section to Boop! or "like" an image.
